How Can You Stop The Bugle From Working?

Tap the spots you think which could! Good luck!

When the cut end of the bottle is closed, no air can be expelled and hence no sound is generated

When a finger is pressed against the balloon covering the mouth of the bottle, there is no vibration and no sound

When the balloon stem is pressed, no air can be blown and there is no sound

When the balloon mouth is pressed, no air flows and there is no vibration or sound
